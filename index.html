Common Questions

Answers to Your
Most Important Questions

Who is eligible for military funeral honors? +
Military funeral honors are available to active duty members, military retirees, members of the Selected Reserve, and eligible U.S. veterans of any war. We help verify eligibility for every veteran we work with. Just reach out and we will walk you through it.
How can I make sure my wishes are honored? +
Through our pre-planning services, every detail of your burial wishes can be documented and securely filed. When the time comes your family will not have to wonder. Everything will be in place exactly as you intended.
Do you also serve non-military family members? +
Yes. We extend our services to non-military family members of veterans as well providing the same level of care, respect, and diligence to every person we serve.
Can you help with burials in national cemeteries? +
Absolutely. We arrange burial in any national cemetery with available space throughout Southern California and handle all pre-registration paperwork on behalf of your family so you do not have to navigate that process alone.
How does your team handle VA benefits? +
We assist in navigating and filing for all veterans burial and memorial benefits making sure you and your family receive every honor and benefit that was earned. Our team knows the process inside and out so you do not have to.
What if I am not sure where to start? +
That is exactly why we are here. Schedule an appointment and we will guide you through every step from understanding what benefits are available to coordinating all final arrangements. You do not have to figure this out alone.
